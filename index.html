Leadership

You only have control over two things in your life: your thoughts and your actions.

To be a true leader, you must master both. Since robots can observe actions, but humans can sense thoughts too.

If your thoughts and actions inspire others to dream more, learn more, do more and become more, you are a leader.

Entrepreneurship

Entrepreneurship is, at its core, the highest form of personal responsibility.

When an entrepreneur fails, there is no room for blaming the government, employees, or customers — only oneself. This is precisely why running a startup is an immense burden, demanding ownership of every success and failure alike.

Yet, being an entrepreneur is extremely satisfying, for it is entrepreneurs who drive progress and change the world.
